## Why this matters now

Most companies are *adding* AI: a chatbot here, a copilot there, a few prompts in Slack. The dashboards look busy. The compounding never arrives.

A few companies are doing something fundamentally different — **re-architecting their operating model so AI is the substrate, not a feature**. They ship faster, retain knowledge across teams, and watch every project make the next one cheaper. **The gap between "added AI" and "AI-first" is now measured in quarters, and widening every month.**

This is the field guide for getting on the right side of that gap.
